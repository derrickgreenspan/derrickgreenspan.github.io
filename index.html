Derrick Greenspan


I am Derrick Greenspan, a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida. I graduated from UCF in Fall 2016 with a Bachelor's in Computer Science, and in Spring 2019 with a Master's in Computer Science. My work focuses on persistent memory (PM) support in Operating Systems and compilers. I am a member of the ARPERS lab at the University of Central Florida.
My advisors are Dr. Mark Heinrich and Dr. Yan Solihin.
